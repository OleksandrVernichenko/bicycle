candy street cruiser

Cassidy is designed in tribute to the raw forms and athletic images of board track racing of the 1920s. This handsome ride is specially reconstructed and fabricated as a crafted objet d’art by Vanguard’s team in Portland. Fitted with leather saddles and a coaster hub brake.

zeth stretched cruiser

From the downhill cruises through San Francisco to circum-navigating the urban grids of Paris, Vara's presence is awe-inspiring. Elegance over exertion is the key. Incorporating aesthetics from classic British racing cars, vespa scooters and the surf culture, the end result is a stretched-out hybrid, exclusively sculpted by Vanguard Designs.
